Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ROI for hydroponic farming?

Our HyperFarm systems offer 42-68% ROI, while SuperFarm systems provide 32-63% ROI, depending on the scale and crops grown.

How much water can I save?

Our hydroponic systems use 90% less water compared to traditional farming methods while maintaining higher yields.

Do you provide training?

Yes, we offer comprehensive training programs for all our systems, including ongoing support and maintenance guidance.

What crops can I grow?

Our systems support a wide variety of crops including leafy greens, herbs, tomatoes, peppers, and many other vegetables and fruits.
