About iRAMA

iRAMA stands for Integrated, Resilient Agricultural Methods for Advancement. We are committed to revolutionizing agriculture through innovative soilless farming solutions.

Who We Are

At iRAMA, we redefine agriculture through cutting-edge hydroponic and soilless farming technologies. Our mission is to create sustainable agricultural solutions that use 90% less water, eliminate pesticides, and produce faster-growing, cleaner crops year-round.

Founded with a vision to transform the agricultural landscape in the Middle East and beyond, we combine innovation with sustainability to address the growing global food security challenges.

Our comprehensive approach encompasses everything from small home units to large-scale commercial farms, ensuring that sustainable agriculture is accessible to everyone.
